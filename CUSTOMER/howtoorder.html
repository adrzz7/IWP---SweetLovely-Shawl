How to Order in SweetLovely Shawls

FOLLOW THE STEP BELOW TO COMPLETE YOUR ORDER :


Step 1: Login / Register

If you have not created your account, click on “Sign Up” button. Enter you information required and click on “Register” button. If you have register with us, click on the "Login As Member" button to login.


Step 2 : Add your desired item to Cart

Choose your selected item, click on “Add to cart” button.


Step 3: Checkout Cart

When you finish shopping click on “ Checkout” to proceed with your purchase.


Step 4: Enter You Details

Add your Name, Email, Phone Number, and Shipping Address first before proceed to payment. Kindly recheck your shipping address.


Step 5: Choose your payment method

Before you choose your payment method, please verify all items & quantity and detail are correctly .

Confirm your billing and shipping address once again and then please choose your payment method.
Before you click the payment method please copy or write it down the “Account Number“

Choose your payment method “Maybank“ or “Cimb Bank“ and click them
Enter your bank detail and choose “Transfers“. You can write or paste the “Account Number“
Enter the amount that we show in your Payment page and finish the final step.

Before you out from bank page “Don't Forget To ScreenShot or Save The Receipt“ and attach it in our “Payment page“.
Finally, after you done attach the payment receipt you can clik the "Submit" button.


Step 7: Successfully proceed with your order

You have now successfully purchase your item. Once we have received your payment, we will arrange to deliver your items soon as possible.
